About me

I received my Bachelor of Science degree in the department of mathematics from the University of Gujarat in, Pakistan. I then received my Master degree COMSATS University Islamabad in, Pakistan.Between August 2022 to December 2022, I was a research assistant at the department of science and humanities, FAST-National University of Computer and Emerging Sciences, Pakistan with Dr. Ahmad Raza Shahid and thanks to Dr. Khadija Farooq. I am a first-year Doctoral student at Laboratory of Optics, Complex Systems and Innovation, at Centro Universitario de los Lagos, University of Guadalajara, Jalisco, Mexico. My Adviosr is Rider Jaimes Reátegui. My dissertation research focused on topological data analysis and time series analysis.
